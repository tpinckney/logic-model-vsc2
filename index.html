Inputs ?
Inputs: Resources invested in your program (staff, funding, materials, partnerships, facilities)

Examples: Budget, personnel, equipment, volunteer time

Activities ?
Activities: What your program does with the inputs (services, processes, interventions)

Examples: Training sessions, counseling, workshops, mentoring

Outputs ?
Outputs: Direct products of your activities (measurable deliverables)

Examples: # people served, # sessions completed, materials distributed

Short-Term Outcomes ?
Short-Term: Immediate changes in participants (knowledge, skills, attitudes) - 1-6 months

Examples: Increased knowledge, improved skills, changed attitudes

Medium-Term Outcomes ?
Medium-Term: Changes in behavior, practices, or conditions - 6 months to 2 years

Examples: Behavior changes, improved practices, policy adoption

Long-Term Outcomes ?
Long-Term: Ultimate impact and systemic changes - 2+ years

Examples: Community transformation, systemic change, sustained impact
