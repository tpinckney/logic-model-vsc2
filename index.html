Include as much of the following as you can:
Who do you serve? e.g., first-generation college students, ages 18–24, at a community college
What is your program's primary goal? e.g., improve retention and graduation rates
What funding stream supports this program? e.g., Title III, TRIO SSS, Title IV, foundation grant
What does success look like in year 3? e.g., 10% increase in graduation rates, 80% of participants persist to second year
